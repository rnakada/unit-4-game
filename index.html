Dragon Ball Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game!


There are four dragon balls below. By clicking on a dragon ball you will add a specific amount of points to your score.


You win the game by matching you total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number


The value of each dragon ball is hidden from you until you click on it


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each dragon ball!
